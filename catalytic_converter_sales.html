Important Warning!

We take a strong stance against the sale of stolen catalytic converters. Engaging in such activities is illegal and unethical. MGB Car Company cooperates fully with law enforcement authorities to combat converter theft and trafficking.

If you are found to be involved in the sale of stolen converters, you will be subject to legal consequences.

The Importance of Proper Documentation

When selling your catalytic converter, it's crucial to provide proper documentation to prove its legitimacy. This includes invoices, receipts, and any certificates of authenticity you may have received when purchasing it originally.

Failure to provide adequate paperwork may result in delays or refusal of the transaction.
